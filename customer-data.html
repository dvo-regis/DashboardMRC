Service Based Tariff

Service-based tariffs price services according to specific usage or features, rather than a fixed fee. This tailored approach charges based on actual consumption, offering transparency but potentially varying costs for users. It aligns expenses with usage, benefitting both consumers and service providers by promoting efficiency and fairness.

This model is commonly employed in various industries such as telecommunications, utilities, and cloud computing. For instance, in telecommunications, service-based tariffs can charge customers based on the number of minutes used, data consumed, or specific services accessed.
